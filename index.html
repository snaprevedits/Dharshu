I'm Sorry

On your birthday — a day that should only carry joy and light — I need to be honest with you. Because you deserve my honesty more than anyone ever has.

I still carry the guilt. The guilt of things I should have said to you and didn't. Words I held back when I shouldn't have. Moments where I could have been better, kinder, more present — and I chose silence instead.

I kept waiting for the right time. And the right time never came. And I'm sorry for that. Truly sorry.

Whatever I should have said — whether it was "I'm sorry," "thank you," "I see you," or just simply "you matter to me more than you know" — I should have said it sooner.

This guilt doesn't leave easily. But I want you to know that on this day, your 19th birthday, I'm choosing to finally say it — even if it's late, even if the moment has passed. Because you deserve to hear it.

You are the best thing that came into my life. And I will spend every day forward trying to be the brother you always deserved. I love you. I'm sorry. And I'm so, so glad you exist.

With every piece of my heart — your brother ❤️
